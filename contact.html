We empower students and create economic opportunity.

Whether you are buying new or used, plain or luxurious, commonplace or rare, trendy or one-of-a-kind – if it exists in the world, it probably is for sale on The Student Exchange. Our mission is to be a student's favorite destination for discovering great value and unique selection.

We give students the platform, solutions, and support they need to purchase or sell their academic items and thrive. We measure our success by our customers' success.
